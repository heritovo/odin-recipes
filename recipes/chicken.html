Description

Chicken is a versatile and widely consumed poultry meat that serves as a staple in cuisines around the world. It is known for its mild flavor and tender texture, making it suitable for a variety of cooking methods including grilling, roasting, frying, and baking. Chicken can be prepared in numerous ways, from simple dishes like roasted chicken with herbs to complex recipes such as chicken curry or coq au vin. It is often paired with a variety of spices, sauces, and side dishes, making it a favorite choice for both everyday meals and special occasions. Additionally, chicken is a good source of protein, vitamins, and minerals, contributing to a balanced diet.

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lemon, sliced (optional)
  • Fresh parsley for garnish (optional)

  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (75째C).
  6. Remove from the oven and let the chicken rest for a few minutes before serving.
